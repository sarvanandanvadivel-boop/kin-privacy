Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 March 2026

Dashy is a dashcam app built by Kin. This policy explains what data the app accesses, why, and what happens to it.

The short version: your footage never leaves your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

Camera

Dashy records video using your device's rear camera. All footage is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is uploaded or transmitted. You control when recording starts and stops.

Microphone

Audio is recorded alongside video as part of the dashcam footage. It is stored locally on your device and never transmitted.

Location (GPS)

GPS data is used in two ways: to display your current speed and coordinates on-screen while recording, and to include your location in the emergency SMS if a crash is detected. Location data is never stored beyond the current session and is never uploaded to any server.

Contacts

You may optionally select one emergency contact from your device's contacts. Their name and phone number are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This information is never transmitted to any server and is only used to send an SMS and place a call during an emergency.

Phone and SMS

In the event of a detected crash, Dashy will automatically send an SMS to your emergency contact containing your GPS location, and place a speakerphone call to the same contact. These actions only occur during an emergency or when you trigger a test from Settings. Dashy never sends SMS or makes calls for any other reason.

Crash Reporting (Sentry)

Dashy uses Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports. If the app crashes, technical information about the crash (such as device model, Android version, and a stack trace) is sent to Sentry's servers. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. No personal data, footage, or location information is included in crash reports. You can review Sentry's privacy policy at sentry.io/privacy.

Data We Do Not Collect

Dashy does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information. We do not serve ads. We do not sell data. We do not share data with third parties beyond the crash reporting described above.

Data Storage

All recordings and settings are stored locally on your device. You can delete recordings at any time through your device's gallery or file manager. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored settings.

Children

Dashy is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
